## 📊 **Supported Data Formats**

### **Required Columns:**
- **Commodity Name** (required)
- **Price** (required)
- **Date** (required)

### **Optional Columns:**
- **Unit** (kg, piece, liter, etc.)
- **Category** (BEEF MEAT PRODUCTS, etc.)

- **Specification** (details, grade, size, etc.)

The system recognizes these variations:
